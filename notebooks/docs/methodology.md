## Core Argument

Newton's Second Law and Transformer Self-Attention
are both dynamic weighting systems applied to sequences.

F = ma states: the force at any moment is proportional
to the acceleration magnitude at that moment.
Large acceleration = large force = dangerous event.

Self-Attention states: the relevance of any timestep
is proportional to its dot product similarity with
all other timesteps in the sequence.
Large relevance score = high attention weight = 
important event.

Both systems independently identify the same timestep
as the most critical moment in the sequence.
